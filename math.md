Previously, as a mathematics student, I specialized in representation theory. During my Bachelors and Masters degrees,
I studied certain invariant arising from representations of simple Lie algebras . My PhD research topic lies in 
the intersection of algebra, representation theory, algebraic geometry and mathematical physics. 
More specifically, I explored shifted Yangians, which are noncommutative versions of 
Coulomb branches. These are spaces appearing in the context of mathematical physics.
